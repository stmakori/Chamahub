Payment Status Guide
Pending

Payment sent via STK push, awaiting M-Pesa confirmation. Check your phone and complete the payment.

Confirmed

Payment successfully processed and confirmed by M-Pesa. Your balance has been updated.

Failed

Payment failed or was cancelled. You may need to retry the payment.

STK Push Payment Process:
  1. Click "Proceed to Payment" and enter your phone number
  2. STK push notification is sent to your phone
  3. Enter your M-Pesa PIN to complete payment
  4. Payment status updates automatically (or manually via debug tool)
